System Requirements
To work Aspia requires a computer:

  • CPU: Pentium 3 or higher.
  • RAM: at least 20 MB of free memory.
  • Hard Drive: at least 10 MB of free disk space.
  • Operating System: Windows XP/2003/2003 R2/Vista/2008/7/2008 R2 (x86 or x64).

It is possible that Aspia will work on other configurations and other computer operating systems, but in this case, the efficiency of the program is not guaranteed.
